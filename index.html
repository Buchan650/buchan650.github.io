Running & Weight Training

Running and weight training are an integral part of my life. I run and lift weights at least three times a week, respectively. For a lot people, fitness is a means to a better physique and a healthier life and although those things are important to me, I love running and weight training because of the physical and mental challenges they provide me. Going for a run or going to the gym when you don't feel like it, getting in one more mile or rep when you feel like you don"t have anymore strength or energy, help me prepare mentally for the life challenges that will eventually come my way(Like learning how to code).
